The bar chart illustrates the development of the book market in four countries in 2014 and also includes a forecast for 2018.
The leading book market according to the chart is in the USA, where are the highest rates. For all of th countries printed books appear to be the most popular compared to e-books both for 2014 and 2018. The most stable situation appears to be in Germany where interest in printed books is predicted to be the same and ebook market is expected to increase by one third. In other three countries the print market is going to decrease as the eBooks gain more investment. According to the chart the printed books in China will not hit a mark lower than 4,2 billion US Dollars while the rate of eBooks is going to double. As for the UK, the book market is not expected to change gradually, though the printed books will probably be left behind by 0,3 billion US Dollars. Though only in the USA non-printed books are expected to overgrow the print by 2018.
Overall, the chart shows two different situations. In the USA and the UK the electronic books will be more popular rather than the printed ones, while in Germany and China the gap is not going to change so quickly.
