The graph illustrates the data on the print and eBook market in four countries in 2014 and provides us with the prediction for 2018.
Overall, that stands out from the chart is that the most progressive development of the print and eBook market for both years, 2014 and 2018, was in the USA. Turning to the details, there is an interesting fact that in Germany the development of print in 2014 and in 2018 was the same, it accounted for six billion US Dollars. Nevertheless, eBook market in Germany didnot reach a peak, only 1,5 Billion US Dollars was spent on the development. The same situation is clearly seen in China. If we look at all countries, China had the lowest point in eBook development. In contrast, the UK had rather stable development in both areas in 2014 and in 2018. However, he development was not very high, only 2,6 Billion US Dollars in 2014 in print area.
