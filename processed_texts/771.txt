The following bar chart demonstrates the percentage of people sorted by age group who use various social networking websites.
Overall, Facebook is the most popular website amongst all the ages as more than a half of respondents in each group prefers it to Instagram and LinkedIn.

According to the bar chart, among young people aged from 18 to 29 Instagram is second most popular website after Facebook with 53 per cent of users. Moreover, younsters tend to use LinkedIn less frequently than older people.
As for the majority of older people aged from 30 to 65+, they are exposed to choosing LinkedIn as second main social network more often. Futhermore, apart from the youngsters, people aged from 30 to 49 are more active users of the following websites than respondents from the last two age groups, whereas people aged 65+ are the least active users of all the ages. This can be proved by the data that in most cases the percentage of people aged 65+ is less than that of other age groups (for instance, Instagram with 6% of older users compared to 11%, 25% and 53% of users of younger generation).
