The bar chart illustrates information about the development of the book market in the USA, Germany, China and the UK and gives us a forecast for 2018.
In general what stands out from the graph is that there is downward trend on the print market and, conversely, there is an upward trend on the eBook market in four countries.
Looking closer at the details, it can be seen that in 2014 the revenues of the USA's print market in 2014 were 10,5 billions US Dollars, but in 2018 they would be only 7,5 billions. As regards eBook maket in the USA, it is noticable that the figure would rise to 2018. Turning to Germany, according to the graph, it is clear that the figure of the print marked would be remained flat. As regards the eBook market, the revenues in 2014 were 1 billion and in 2018 it is predicted 1,5. In China figure of the eBook market rises too. The revenues of China eBook market doubled. Finally, there would be a downward trend in the print market in the UK and an upward trend in the eBook market.
It is interesting to note that the USA has the most efficient print and eBook market. China's and Germany's book markets are not so efficient.
