The bar chart illustrates the percentage use of Instagram, Facebook and LinkedIn by adults in the USA by age group.
It can be seen from the chart that Facebook is the most popular social network. The majority of adults use it everyday. 87% of youth spend their time at facebook. Moreover, 73% of people from 30 till 49 use use it, while only 25% of them use instagram. Nevertheless, Instagram is also used frequently by youth 53%, though it is not popular among elder people. In particular, only 6 % of people at their 60s use this social platform. There is a huge difference in comparison with usage of Facebook. Not so many people use LinkedIn, adults till 29 years old and people after 64 spend the same time using LinkedIn.
According the graph, LinkedIn has approximately the equal audience, while Instagram is a major social network for young people. To sum up, Facebook is a social network, where people from all ages can communicate.
