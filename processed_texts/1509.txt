This barchart illustrates the development of the print and eBook market in four countries in 2014 with a forecast for 2018. Units are measured in billions of US Dollars.
Overall, we may see that the USA have the most developed book market of all four countries, in terms of billions of US Dollars. Besides, one can note that in all the countries on the chart, except the UK, the volume of sold print books dramatically differs from the volume of sold eBooks. So, as for print books, the USA have the highest points on the chart, both for 2014 and 2018 forecast. The UK stands for the lowest point on the chart for this type of books, both for 2014 and 2018 forecast.
And what concernes the eBook market, again, the USA have the highest points, both for 2014 and 2018 forecast. But here China stands for the lowest points in eBook market, both for 2014 and 2018 forecast.
