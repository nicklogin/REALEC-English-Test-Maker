The given bar chart presents the proportion of money spent on the development of the book market in USA, Germany, China and UK in 2014 and gives a prediction for 2018.
As it can be seen from the chart, the print market trend for USA, UK and China is likely to decrease significantly by 2018 and reach 7,5 billion dollars while in Germany this proportion will remain stable. at the mark of 6.
It should pointed out that there is an opposite proportion for ebooks market. We observe a rising trend in all the countries given, however, the number of money spent on the development of eBook market in 2018 is much lower than on traditional printmarket.
Clearly, usage of print books is likely to reduce considerably in the developed countries by 2018. However, the role of eBook market will significantly increase.
