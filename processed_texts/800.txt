Nowadays a great number of people are involved on sport activities. They may participate in them or be a fan, but still sports have an essential role in their lives.
Watching sport events has an enormous popularity, but there exists an opinion that it is a vain way of spending time. Frankly speaking, I am inclined to deny such belief.

To begin with, sports are the vital part of human life and various sport events can arouse the desire to maintain the physical fit. As a consequence, people will be far more motivated to have good health. Furthermore, such great sport events as the Olympic Games, for instance, can reduce tension between nations. Moreover sport event stimulates patriotic feelings and a pride for the country.



To draw up a conclusion, I would like to say that watching sports join people. The find common points of interst and become more frienly to each other.
