The rate was reduced in Latin America, it was at 6,8% high in 2014 and then it felf slowly to 5,8%, so it follows Middle Eeast in reduction of unemployments

South Asia and Worldwide had the same trend as North Africa and their data didn’t changed between 2014 and 2015. S. Asia stayed at 3,9% and their rate was better than every other region had. Worldwide kept 5,9% of unemployment rate and it was on a good position too.
