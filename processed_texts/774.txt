The system of receiving a guaranteed income every month by citizens has been suggested by political parties. This suggestion emerged in debates by proponent and opponents of such system. In this essay we will discuss some advantages and disadvantages of this idea.
On the one hand, citizens of the country will definitely benefit from receiving every month sable income. Such system will be a great help not only for homeless and big families but for people who faced temporal problems. For example, some man has lost his job and some kind of black line started in his life, this monthly sum of money can help him to overcome this hurdles.
On the other hand, where government is going to get money for this? All the money that government spends on social problems is payed from working citizens’ budget. Thus it means that such system requires increasing taxes, which will affect lower classes.
To conclude, the system of receiving a guaranteed income every month has some benefits, though disadvantages is definitely outweigh its advantages.
