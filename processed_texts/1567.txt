The bar graph gives data about book market, both print and electronic, in four different countries in 2014 and predictes changes in market for 2018.
Given four countries can be sepparated into 2 groups by the tendencies they follow. Group 1 consists of USA and UK, where in 2014 printed books are leading, but by the 2018 the ebook sales will increase and become the leader. Group 2 is Germany and China where both in 2014 and 2018 print is the main type of books which rules the market. In all contries we can see a trend of developing ebook market and declining printed one. Another tendency is the overall prediction of crisis or, at least, fall in sales in book market in every given country. It is depicted less obvious in UK and USA, even thought, there is a clear fall in Sales in Germany and China, in other words; in non-english speaking countries.
